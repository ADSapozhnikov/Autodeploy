About us

Quality without compromise

Making impossible - is not such a great problem, if you know where to start... - Max Frei

In autumn in 2004, after long and difficult negotiations between La Marzocco and Golden Bar companies the dealership contract was signed. That was the beginning of deliveries of La Marzocco espresso machines in Ukraine. At that time the word "La Marzocco" and "barista" was practicully unknown even to restaurateurs, not to mention the people not related to the restaurant business…

Nobody believed in La Marzocco’s success in Ukraine. Even the La Marzocco company concidered ukrainian market to be weak and not ready for such equipment. But, fortunately for all Ukrainian baristas and coffee lovers, the Golden Bar team truly believed in the success of its undertaking. Step by step, year after year, we were developing… It was hard, but we moved on. More and more La Marzocco equipment made coffee lovers happy across Ukraine.

Today the BFB company is a part of the Golden Bar Group that continues to develop the market of products for barista. It offers not only La Marzocco coffee machines but also other wonderful goods and accessories that, we hope, will help barista in his not easy, but such a great job!

Has more than 20 years experience in the HoReCa segment and hundreds successful projects throughout Ukraine. The quality and stability in everything is his life credo.
